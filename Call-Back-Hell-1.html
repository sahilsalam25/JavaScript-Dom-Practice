Call Back Hell...in JavaScript...
Callback hell, also known as "pyramid of doom," is a situation in JavaScript programming where multiple asynchronous operations are nested within each other, resulting in code that is difficult to read, understand, and maintain. This nesting occurs when callbacks are used to handle asynchronous tasks, and those tasks are dependent on the completion of other tasks.
